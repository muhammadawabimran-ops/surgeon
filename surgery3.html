Aortic Aneurysm
Repair

Life-saving intervention to repair hazardous dilation of the aorta. We utilize both open surgical grafting and Endovascular Aortic Repair (EVAR) depending on anatomical suitability.

The Risk

Untreated aneurysms can rupture, causing life-threatening internal bleeding. Elective repair eliminates this risk.

EVAR Technique

A minimally invasive option where a stent graft is inserted via the groin arteries to reinforce the weak aortic wall from the inside.

Recovery

EVAR: 1-2 Days Hospital Stay Open: 5-7 Days Hospital Stay Rigorous follow-up imaging ensures graft stability.
