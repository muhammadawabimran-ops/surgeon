Mitral Valve
Repair

Preserving the patient's native valve is always our priority. This procedure reconstructs the mitral valve leaflets and annulus to correct regurgitation without the need for a prosthetic replacement.

Repair vs Replacement

Repair provides better long-term survival, preserves heart function, and avoids the need for lifelong blood thinners associated with mechanical valves.

Robotic Assistance

We utilize the Da Vinci surgical system for enhanced 3D visualization and millimeter-precision instrument control through small incisions.

Outcome

95% of patients are free from re-operation at 10 years. Recovery is expedited due to the minimally invasive approach.
